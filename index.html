Welcome to U&MUN 2018

Dear Delegates,

It is with utmost humility and hope that I welcome you all to an unforgettable experience of debating issues of pertinence as the future of the global community.

We, at U&MUN, believe in the power of the ideals of liberalism; the propagation of war can be curbed through dialogue and compromise. At the very heart of this conference, lies the idea that in today's day and age, the international sociopolitical scenario is of utmost importance in deciding the fate of the community that our ancestors have built over the millennia.

Consider yourself fortunate for having the opportunity to engage in diplomatic endeavors, even if only stimulated, in today's time. The world is perpetually on the brink of war and peace simultaneously. Nations hold the power to lead the world into eternal darkness or indulge in securing the very existence of a future to look forward to.

The idea of peaceful coexistence has never seemed bleaker; Yemen is a failed state, the Rohingya Muslims flee from one nation to the other in search of asylum, South America has fallen prey to political instability and many African states struggle to feed their people. Despite this rampant turmoil, here we are: yet to witness a third world war despite being on the brink of it several times since the Cold War. This is indicative of the presence of a potential. A potential to contain violence, to buffer hatred, to sustain the absence of conflict. At U&MUN 2.0, we plan to harness this potential.

With the highest degree of sincerity, I invite you to explore the diplomatic, academic sides within you to create a world that is Bigger and Better.

Sameera Singh
Secretary-General
