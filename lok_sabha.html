Lok Sabha

Analysing the findings of the Mandal Commission and the repercussions of its implementation


Director: Parth Pareek

Vice Directors: Aashish Choudhary


The state v/s Indira sawhney The reservations, the most controversial yet legal step taken by any government that ever came into power. The Mandal commission was set up by our former prime minister Shri Moraji Desai, its mandate was to identify the socially or educationally backward classes and submit its findings to the government.the report said that 27% reservation to be given to OBC i.e other backward classes. Until 1990 this report didn't become a law.when Shri V.P Singh decided to use this report in his benefit, he created a vote bank which accounted for about half of India's population and later that year the report was made into a law. This law led to bloodshed in all aspects across the nation. Riots,revolts and protests increased day by day. Even the supreme Court stated it was legal. We want the delegates to step into the shoes of the politicians of that time and take decisions,but remember consequences stretch far longer then what we can imagine.
