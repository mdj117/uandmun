Drug Enforcement Administration

Capturing the Medelin Cartel


Director: Advya Shukla

Vice Directors: Aadesh Chhabra


Ruthless, fierce and downright merciless, the Medellín Cartel has riddled the streets of Columbia with fear. By smuggling thousands of tons of cocaine throughout the American continents, Kingpin Pablo Escobar has brought shame upon the Columbian police and the DEA alike. But after the years of drug-related violence and the deaths of hundreds of innocent people, the time is nigh’ to stop this. The DEA has listed the capture of Pablo Escobar as a top priority and the search for his body, dead or alive, is on now more than ever. The committee is required to step up, work together and hunt down the man who has struck fear in the hearts of millions before he slips away forever.
